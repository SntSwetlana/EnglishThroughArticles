🧠 Reading of the Week #2

Hearing Loss and the Hidden Risks to Health

Level: C1–C2
IELTS Target: 7.5–8.5
Source: New Scientist

Most people think hearing loss is simply an unavoidable part of ageing.

However, growing evidence suggests that it may be linked to much more than difficulty hearing conversations.

The article explores several important questions:

• Why is hearing loss associated with dementia, cardiovascular disease and social isolation?
• Could hearing problems be an early warning sign of more serious health conditions?
• How might gene therapy, stem cells and new pharmaceuticals transform treatment in the future?

This is a challenging scientific article packed with advanced academic vocabulary and IELTS Passage 3-style arguments.
